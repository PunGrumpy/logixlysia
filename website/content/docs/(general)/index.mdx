Logixlysia is a logging plugin for [Elysia.js](https://elysiajs.com/) that provides a simple and flexible way to log requests and responses in your Elysia applications. It is designed to be easy to use and highly configurable, allowing you to customize the logging format, level, and output.

## Why use Logixlysia?

Integrating logging into your Elysia application can help you monitor and debug your application more effectively. Logixlysia provides a simple and consistent way to log requests and responses, making it easier to track down issues and understand how your application is behaving.
It also allows you to customize the logging format and level, so you can focus on the information that is most important to you.
